A little about me

Like for most people, 2020 created an incredible life shift. After spending most of the last decade out on the baseball field, traveling up and down the East Coast coaching, suddenly I was doing everything from home after having just moved to the Pacific Northwest. However, I will be greatful for 2020 for one thing: it gave me the opportunity to dive into the universe of coding!

Having always been intrigued by it, coding often seemed like an area too vast to truly explore, but once I had the opportunity to dabble in it, I quickly found out that I loved it! Not only did it provide me the mental exercise of connecting seemingly disparate ideas, it constantly took my mind in clever new directions and made me rethink the way I approached life offline as well. As someone who has always been very analytical, it's given me the opportunity to explore and strenghten my creativity. It's also opened my eyes to the endless possibilities, and solutions that coding can provide. Needless to say, I'm incredibly excited about my career, and hope to be able to make other lives better by contributing to exciting new innovations.
